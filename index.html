Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

All operations are performed locally on your browser, and no email content or browsing activity is accessed or stored.

The extension only injects AI-generated content into Gmail upon your request. It communicates with an API (hosted on Render.com) for email generation purposes only.

No user-identifiable information is collected or transmitted.
